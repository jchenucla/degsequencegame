Edges

How many graphs can you create?

A New York Times inspired game by: Joy Chen (UCLA) and Sungmin Park (ASU)

×

How to Play

Connect the dots based on the degree sequence shown on the screen before the timer runs out!

1. Click on the circle that you wish to connect.

2. Click on another circle that you wish to connect the clicked circle to.

3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 to create the graph before the timer runs out!

4. Cick the "Check" button to verify if the graph matches the degree sequence and to gain higher points.

--------------------------------

Tip: Use the "Undo" button to undo your last move.

Tip: Visit again tomorrow to maintain your Streak!

Tip: The faster you complete the sequence, the more points you earn!
